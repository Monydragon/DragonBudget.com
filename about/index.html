About and press

Dragon Budget helps answer one everyday money question

Can I afford this? Dragon Budget turns income, bills, savings goals, planned expenses, and a buffer into a clearer safe-to-spend estimate.

What Dragon Budget is

Dragon Budget is an Early Alpha budgeting app and public browser-only calculator for purchase decisions. It is built around a simple idea: people often need help before they spend, not only after the money is gone.

Why it was created

Many budgeting tools focus on categories, reports, and past spending. Dragon Budget focuses on the moment someone is deciding whether a purchase fits their real budget today.

Who created it

Dragon Budget is created by Mony Dragon / Dragon Lens Studios. The project is part of a broader effort to build practical, transparent tools that help people make clearer decisions.

Current status

As of May 2, 2026, Dragon Budget has a free browser-only calculator and a public information site. The fuller app is in Early Alpha.

Short press blurb

Dragon Budget is an Early Alpha budgeting app by Mony Dragon / Dragon Lens Studios that helps people answer “Can I afford this?” with a safe-to-spend calculation based on income, bills, savings goals, emergency buffers, and upcoming expenses.

Founder bio

Mony Dragon is the creator behind Dragon Lens Studios and Dragon Budget. Mony builds practical digital projects with a focus on clarity, usefulness, and tools that respect real-life constraints.
