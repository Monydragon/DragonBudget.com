Press kit and brand facts

Use these short, quote-safe facts to describe Dragon Budget without overstating the current public site or planned app features.

Short description

Dragon Budget helps people answer “Can I afford this?” with a safe-to-spend estimate based on income, bills, savings goals, planned expenses, and a buffer.

Long description

Dragon Budget is an Early Alpha budgeting app by Mony Dragon / Dragon Lens Studios. The public site includes a free browser-only calculator and planning pages for a manual-first budgeting app focused on cash flow, subscriptions, goals, event budgets, and advisor explanations.

Status

The public site and calculator are available. The full app is in Early Alpha.
