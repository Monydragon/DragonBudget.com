Bills and subscriptions

Dragon Budget plans to make recurring money commitments visible before spending decisions: bills, subscriptions, renewals, reminders, and possible savings.

Bills

Track due dates, amount, account, autopay, paid/unpaid state, reminder settings, notes, and payment history.

Subscriptions

Track renewal date, billing cycle, monthly and yearly totals, price history, cancellation links, and status.

Subscription Watch

Planned summary that highlights possible savings if subscriptions are paused, canceled, or reviewed.

Current status

The static website includes a subscription savings calculator and guide content. Full bill and subscription tracking is planned for the app MVP.
