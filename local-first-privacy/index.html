Product direction

Local-first privacy

Dragon Budget’s product direction is manual-first and privacy-aware: useful without bank sync, clear about storage, and export-friendly.

Manual-first

The first app version should be useful with manually entered accounts, transactions, bills, and subscriptions.

Local storage goal

The app plan favors local SQLite persistence with repository abstractions before any cloud sync.

Future boundaries

Accounts, analytics, payments, cloud sync, and bank connections require updated privacy language before launch.
