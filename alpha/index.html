What testers can help with

Dragon Budget is being shaped around the practical money moments that are hard to judge from a bank balance alone.

  • Does the safe-to-spend result feel clear and useful?
  • Which bills, subscriptions, or planned expenses are missing?
  • Would cash-flow planning help before payday?
  • What should the app explain before asking for trust?

Simple email collection

The signup link opens an email addressed to alpha@dragonbudget.com. That keeps the public site simple while collecting a tester list for alpha invitations and occasional product updates.

Do not send bank logins, account numbers, or private financial details. Feedback should stay practical and safe to share.

What joining means

By asking to join, you are giving Dragon Budget permission to contact you about alpha testing, feedback requests, and occasional product updates or promotions. You can ask to be removed at any time.

The Early Alpha is not a finished financial product. Dragon Budget is a budgeting aid, not financial, legal, tax, credit, investment, or debt advice.
