Brand voice

Dragon Budget should sound clear, protective, direct, mature, slightly warm, and never shame-based.

Say

“Based on your current entries...” “Here is what is already spoken for.” “This may be a good moment to wait.”

Avoid

Shame, panic, fake certainty, childish mascot jokes, and aggressive upselling.

Personality

A calm guardian of the user’s budget: useful, direct, and respectful.
