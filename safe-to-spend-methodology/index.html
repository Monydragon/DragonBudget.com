Safe-to-spend methodology

Safe-to-spend is an estimate of money left after current balance, upcoming income, bills, planned expenses, savings, goals, and a chosen buffer are accounted for.

Inputs

The static calculator uses current balance, upcoming income, bills due, planned expenses, savings goal contribution, emergency buffer, and purchase amount.

Assumptions

The result assumes the entries are complete and that no unexpected expense arrives before the next pay period. The planned app should add confidence levels and risk flags when information is missing.

Risk flags

  • Low remaining buffer after purchase.
  • Missing bill or subscription entries.
  • Irregular income that is not guaranteed.
  • Large planned events close to bill due dates.

What the calculation does not know

Dragon Budget cannot know future emergencies, unentered bills, bank delays, overdraft rules, debt terms, or personal priorities unless the user enters that context.

Dragon Budget is a budgeting aid, not financial advice. Methodology pages describe planning logic, not guaranteed outcomes.
