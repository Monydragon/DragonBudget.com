Product direction

Advisor methodology

Dragon Advisor should answer from local budget entries, cite the entries it used, explain calculations plainly, and avoid acting like a generic chatbot.

Grounding rules

  • Use account balances, bills, subscriptions, transactions, budgets, goals, and planned items entered by the user.
  • Say when data is missing instead of guessing.
  • Cite the entries behind the answer.
  • Use wording like “Based on your current entries...”

Limits

The advisor should not guarantee outcomes, provide professional financial advice, or infer sensitive facts from nowhere.

Privacy direction

The planned first app version should prefer local data first. Any future cloud or hosted processing service must be documented before launch.
