Terms for Dragon Budget

These terms describe the current public site, free calculator, and Early Alpha tester list. They should be reviewed before a production app launch.

Last updated: May 2, 2026.

This page is not a substitute for legal review.

Use of the site

You may use the Dragon Budget public site and calculator for personal budgeting exploration. Do not misuse the site, attempt to disrupt it, or present its output as guaranteed financial advice.

Budgeting aid only

Dragon Budget is a budgeting aid, not financial, legal, tax, credit, investment, or debt advice. The calculator depends on the numbers you enter and cannot know every future expense or event.

Early Alpha communications

If you ask to join the Early Alpha list, Dragon Budget may contact you about testing invitations, feedback requests, and occasional product updates or promotions. You can ask to be removed at any time.

No guarantee

Results are estimates for planning. Dragon Budget does not guarantee that a purchase will be affordable or that your financial situation will match the calculator output.

Changes

These starter terms may change as the product develops, especially if accounts, payments, cloud sync, or bank connections are added. For support questions, email support@dragonbudget.com.
