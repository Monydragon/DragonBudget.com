Pricing philosophy

Transparent, accessible pricing

The free calculator is available now. Dragon Budget is exploring adaptive pricing so planned premium features can stay affordable for people with different financial situations.

What adaptive pricing means here

Dragon Budget is exploring adaptive pricing so premium features can stay affordable for people with different financial situations. The goal is not to punish higher income users. The goal is to keep the app accessible while letting people pay within a realistic range.

Pricing guardrails

  • The user sees the price before paying.
  • No surprise billing.
  • Easy cancellation must be part of the paid product.
  • No sale of financial data.
  • Any pricing calculation should be transparent enough to understand.

Current status

As of May 2, 2026, this public site offers the free browser-based calculator. Paid premium features are planned, not currently sold through this public site.

Example ranges

Example pricing ranges are planning examples only: free for public tools, a possible low-cost range around $1 to $5 per month, and a possible higher range around $10 to $20 per month for users who choose to support premium features. No active billing exists here.

Pricing details may change before launch. This page is meant to document the current philosophy and guardrails honestly.
