Brand system

Mascot usage

The Dragon Budget mascot is a mature blue dragon guardian: sleek, intelligent, protective, and never chibi, goofy, or childlike.

Usage rules

  • Use the blue dragon-head PNG for favicon, app icon, header marks, advisor avatar, and compact mascot moments.
  • Keep the dragon mature, protective, intelligent, and fantasy-fintech instead of cute or toy-like.
  • Keep finance UI professional and let the dragon support, not dominate.
  • Avoid baby proportions, joke-heavy mascot copy, and generic SVG placeholder art.
