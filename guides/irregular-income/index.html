Budgeting with irregular income

When income varies, safe-to-spend should use conservative income estimates, visible due dates, and a buffer that protects essentials.

Use conservative income

Count money that is likely and timed clearly. Treat uncertain income as a bonus, not a guarantee.

Separate fixed and flexible costs

Rent, utilities, debt minimums, and groceries come before optional spending.

Example

If you might earn $900 or $1,300 this week, plan from $900 until the higher amount arrives.
