Privacy

Privacy for Dragon Budget

The current Dragon Budget calculator runs in your browser and does not store or transmit your financial inputs by default. The Early Alpha list only collects contact details people choose to send.

Last updated: May 2, 2026.

This page should be reviewed before production launch, especially before adding accounts, analytics, cloud sync, payment processing, or bank connections.

Calculator inputs

The Can I Afford This calculator runs in the browser. It does not send your entered financial numbers to a server by default, and it does not store them in local storage or a database.

Theme preference

The site may save your light or dark mode preference in your browser. This is not a financial input and is only used to keep the site in the mode you selected.

Early Alpha tester list

If you use the Early Alpha signup link, your email app may send your email address, name, and optional feedback to alpha@dragonbudget.com. That information is used to invite testers, ask for feedback, and send occasional Dragon Budget product updates or promotions. You can ask to be removed at any time.

Analytics

No analytics are included in this public version. If analytics are added later, this page should be updated to explain what is collected and why.

Accounts, cloud sync, and bank connections

The current site does not provide accounts, cloud sync, or bank connections. If those features are added later, the privacy policy must be updated before launch.

Contact and updates

Privacy expectations should stay visible as Dragon Budget develops. For support or privacy requests, email support@dragonbudget.com.
