Product direction

Planned app features

Dragon Budget is planned as a manual-first budgeting app with clear safe-to-spend, cash-flow planning, subscription control, goals, reports, and data ownership.

Dashboard

Net worth, safe-to-spend, upcoming bills, spending, subscription watch, and advisor tips.

Cash Flow

Projected balance timeline for paydays, bills, subscriptions, planned expenses, and goals.

Transactions

Manual entry, filters, categories, payees, notes, recurring markers, and exports.

Budgets

Category targets, warning thresholds, rollover options, notes, and progress.

Bills

Recurring and one-time bills with paid status, reminders, due dates, and history.

Subscriptions

Renewal dates, cost totals, price history, cancellation links, and possible savings.

Goals

Savings and debt goals with progress charts and next actions.

Insights and Reports

Plain-language summaries for cash flow, categories, subscriptions, goals, and trends.

Dragon Advisor

Local-data answers with cited entries and transparent calculations.

Accounts

Manual account balances, account types, notes, archives, and history.

Export and Backup

CSV, JSON, local backup, and user-owned data workflows.

Premium

Planned non-pushy premium architecture with transparent pricing guardrails.
